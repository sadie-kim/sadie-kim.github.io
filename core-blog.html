Using the evidence gathered, describe your strengths and limitations


Perfectionism is both my strength and limitation at the same time. Because I want to do well, I work hard, but because I struggle to accept anything below perfect, I tend to neglect the timeframe given to each task and fail to meet the deadline. At the moment, I am struggling with meeting the deadline. I need to learn to apply ‘just in time’ learning as we are learning to code and acknowledge that it is impossible to have perfect knowledge when learning to code.

Emotional Intelligence

May 12

What is Emotional Intelligence?

It’s an ability to correctly recognise one’s own and other’s emotions and the ability to appropriately express and communicate emotions.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ is a short for ‘Intelligence Quotient’. It is a numerical value given to measure people’s intelligence level. IQ is innate, but EQ is acquired.

Why is it important?

EQ is important because a huge part of working especially in most industries is working in a team with a group of people. One’s EQ can translate to someone’s ability to work well with others in a team.
